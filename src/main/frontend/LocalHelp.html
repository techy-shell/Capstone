Find your local support services

We all know there is help out there, but sometimes it can be overwhelming trying to find this help. More importantly, it isn't always easy to find the help that is close to where you live.
You can use the tool below to enter your City - this will then provide you with local charities or organisations that are local to you. These are places that aim to provide you with financial support and advice to help with the current Cost Of Living Situation.





National support services

We hope you have been able to utilise the charity or organisation to the left, which is local to your area.
However, take a look below to see what help and support is available on a national level, regardless of where you are based in the country.


 Cost of Living Payment - you may be entitled to this payment based on the benefits and tax credits that you receive.

 Warm Home Discount Scheme - you could get £150 off your electricity bill for winter 2022 to 2023; it’s a one-off discount on your electricity bill, between October 2022 and March 2023. You may be able to get the discount on your gas bill instead if your supplier provides you with both gas and electricity and you’re eligible.

 NEA - for advice on energy bills and how to keep warm at home, contact National Energy Action, who are the UK's leading fuel poverty charity.

 Money Helper - help and advice on setting up a new home, care and disability, debt and divorce/separation.

 Help with Childcare Costs - you could be eligible to 30 hours pf free childcare a week for families with 3 and 4 year old children. You can also get up to £500 every 3 months (up to £2000 a year) for each of your children to help with childcare costs.

 Help with Transport Costs - there are many ways you can save money on bus, rail and other transport methods.
